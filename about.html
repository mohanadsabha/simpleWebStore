Our History

Founded in 2012 in a small departmant in Gaza, we have grown in size and presence around the world, and the ability to tackle complex international development challenges.

With over $2.5 million in annual revenues, 2,000+ Palestinian employees, and helping create over 100,000 jobs around the world across their supplier base, RobotStore has very ambitious expansion plans for the future. This includes opening additional branches across the world, as well as continuing to introduce new and innovative products across all major product lines.
